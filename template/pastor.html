Our Pastor

Pastor
Keith Locke
Pastor Keith Locke is a child of the Most High God, a husband, father, grandfather and a Christian friend. he gave his life to Christ in his early teenage years while he was attending a Moffettville Baptist Church in the small town of Wilmer, Alabama. By God’s grace after years of walking in my comfort zone, he became an active member in church and participating in awesome Sunday School class. he was ordained as a deacon in 2004 at Ebenezer Missionary Baptist Church in Mobile, Alabama. he was youth Sunday School Teacher, Wednesday night youth Bible study, and served as church clerk. He also obtained student and teacher evangelism training from Childhood Evangelism Fellowship.

This is a true saying,If a man desires the office of a bishop,he desireth a good work.1 Timothy 3:1 he received a license to preach after a noted sermon December 3, 2017.Pastor Locke was asked was asked to pastor the Redeem Christian Church of God Jesus House Mobile January 2018 and was later ordained as a Pastor by our General Overseer during the RCCGNA Annual Convention in Greenville, Texas. he has held the responsibilities as an industrial engineer, project manager, sports coach, park to college, and teacher from assistant to college instructor.He earned a bachelor of science degree in industrial engineering in 2004.He has been given the opportunity to share the gospel many people of various ages. he and Sister Locke have been married since 1990.They have two sons, Keith (Rafiqa) and Kendall and one daughter, Danielle (Delano),and 5 grandchildren.
